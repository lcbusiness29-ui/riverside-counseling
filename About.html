About Us

At Riverside Counseling, we believe that healing begins with connection. Our practice was founded on the principle that every individual deserves a safe, supportive space to explore their thoughts, emotions, and experiences. Located in the heart of Northfield, Minnesota, we serve our community with compassion, professionalism, and a deep commitment to mental wellness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals, couples, and families to navigate life’s challenges with resilience and clarity. We strive to foster emotional well-being through evidence-based therapeutic approaches, personalized care, and a respectful, inclusive environment.

Who We Serve

We welcome clients from all walks of life, including adolescents, college students, adults, and seniors. Whether you're facing anxiety, depression, grief, trauma, relationship issues, or simply seeking personal growth, our therapists are here to support you on your journey.

Our Approach

Our team integrates a variety of therapeutic modalities tailored to each client’s needs. These include cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based practices, trauma-informed care, and narrative therapy. We believe in collaborative treatment planning and encourage clients to take an active role in shaping their healing process.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Elaine Harper, PhD – Licensed Psychologist Dr. Harper specializes in trauma recovery, anxiety disorders, and life transitions. She brings over 15 years of clinical experience and a gentle, insightful presence to her work.

Jonah Reyes, LICSW – Clinical Social Worker Jonah focuses on family systems, adolescent mental health, and grief counseling. His approach is grounded in empathy, cultural awareness, and practical support.

Talia Nguyen, LMFT – Marriage & Family Therapist Talia works with couples and families to improve communication, rebuild trust, and navigate conflict. She is passionate about relational healing and resilience.

Our Space

Our office is designed to be a calming retreat from the stress of daily life. With warm lighting, comfortable seating, and a view of the Cannon River, we aim to create an atmosphere that promotes reflection and peace. We offer both in-person and teletherapy sessions to accommodate your preferences and needs.

Community Commitment

Riverside Counseling is proud to be part of the Northfield community. We collaborate with local schools, healthcare providers, and nonprofits to promote mental health awareness and access. We also offer workshops, support groups, and outreach programs throughout the year.

Confidentiality & Ethics

We adhere to the highest standards of confidentiality and professional ethics. Your privacy and trust are central to our practice, and we are committed to maintaining a secure and respectful therapeutic environment.

Get in Touch

If you’re curious about therapy or ready to begin, we invite you to reach out. Our team is here to answer your questions, schedule a consultation, and help you take the next step toward healing.
